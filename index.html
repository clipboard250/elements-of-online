The Elements of Online

H
Honesty
Say what’s true. No dressing it up.
Cl
Clarity
Write with purpose and precision.
Tr
Transparency
Show sources. Share context.
Re
Respect
Treat your audience like people.
Ac
Attribution
Credit work and ideas you build on.
Co
Consistency
Keep tone and style aligned.
Ed
Editing
Polish until it’s clear and concise.
Su
Substance
Provide value, not fluff.
In
Inclusivity
Write so anyone can engage.
Ex
Experimentation
Try new things, learn, iterate.
